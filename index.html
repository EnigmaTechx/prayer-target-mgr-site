Welcome

Prayer Target Manager, or PT MGR for short, is an online tool to help keep the average Christian keep track of their prayer life. According to Matthew 26:41, Jesus spent at least one hour praying for the will of God when He was about to be crucified. As people who claim to follow Christ, praying for at least one hour is an excellent standard to follow for any situation in our lives. We must also use scriptures to back our prayer topics because the Word of God says, "anything ye shall ask in my name, the Father may give it to you" (John 15:16). We believe Jesus is the living Word of God, so it is wise to use the Word when making requests to God Almighty.

This productivity tool helps you create prayer topics, set goals i.e., number of hours you want to pray, and lets you record new hours you pray towards your goal. Give it a try.

Pray without ceasing. - 1 Thessalonians 5:17
